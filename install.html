Install the Digital Harms app

Installing a web app is easy, but it might be new to you. That's okay! Here's a look at how you can install this app for most device types.

iOS (iPhone)

1. Click the share button in Safari.

2. Select "Add to Home Screen".

3. Follow the prompts / click Add.

This will place an easy-to-use button on your phone screen. The button looks like this and is called “Digital Harms”.

Android

1. Click on the three dots at the top right of the screen.

2. Select "Add to Home screen".

3. This will open a box that says "Install app". Click the "Install" button.

3. Drag the app icon to your home screen or click "Add to home screen".

This will place an easy-to-use button on your phone screen. The button looks like this and is called “Digital Harms”.

If you’re having any trouble installing the app, please let us know via the technical support page
